Xianjun 显君

Animated Short Film

Chosen at birth to be freed of fate’s endless spiral, Xiǎnjūn lived towards nihility. Any who’d claim him as their own would be reduced to fate’s arachnid vassal, as his loving mother once did.

Though sworn to nihility, Xiǎnjūn lived through abundance, fate’s vassals at his beck and call preparing him for singularity.

Once of age, Xiǎnjūn was summoned to realize his destiny. To abandon his flesh, be devoured by the vassals, and be released.

Only in this moment did he see that fate was bound to itself. Life, death, space, and time, the very order of this world leached off fate, as fate did to him

“And thus I reject your design, and so shall I weave my own... I will rend this world asunder to free you from yourself, as you did... for me.”
